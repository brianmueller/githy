The Goal Is To Hook You

Your Data
About
  • GITHY is a simulation of a social media site.
  • Our goal is to make you aware of how social media companies are tracking your interaction and using that data to manipulate you into using their app even more. You are the product. Your attention is their greatest asset.
  • This site does not save any of your data.
  • It was created by @Brian Mueler and @Daiana Munoz as a final project for an Ethics course as part of Hunter College's Computer Science Education Advanced Certificate Program.
  • If you share GITHY on social media, you're spreading awareness about the goal of social media. Thanks!
  • If you don't share GITHY, you're aware. Thanks!
